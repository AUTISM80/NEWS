The sea is cleaned of plastic with new inventions!

The 25-year-old inventor Boyan Slat from the Netherlands, intends to clean up the rivers and oceans of plastic with his ingenious inventions together with his employees in the organization The Ocean Cleanup. It is expected to be able to implement the remediation in five years. The Ocean Cleanup has already started work in Malaysia, among others.
